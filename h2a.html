What is the H-2A Visa?

The H-2A visa program enables U.S. agricultural employers to hire foreign workers for temporary or seasonal jobs when thereâ€™s a shortage of domestic labor. Updated for 2025, this program includes streamlined processes to ensure compliance and efficiency.

Benefits of the H-2A Program

Reliable Workforce

Secure skilled workers during peak seasons to keep your operations running smoothly.

Legal Compliance

Stay compliant with federal labor laws through a structured visa program.

Cost Savings

Benefit from exemptions on Social Security and Medicare taxes for H-2A workers.

Employer Responsibilities

  • Job Eligibility: Prove a shortage of U.S. workers for temporary/seasonal roles.
  • Wages: Pay the highest of AEWR, state/federal minimum, or prevailing wage (2025 rates apply).
  • Housing & Travel: Provide free housing and cover transportation costs.
  • Safety Standards: Meet updated DOL health and safety requirements.
