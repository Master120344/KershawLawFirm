Expert H-2A & H-2B Visa Guidance

Navigate complex visa pathways with confidence. Secure the workforce essential for your success.

Securing reliable seasonal labor is crucial for operational success. The complex H-2A and H-2B visa processes require strategic navigation. The Kershaw Law Firm transforms this challenge into your advantage, ensuring you have the essential workforce when you need it most.

H-2A Visa

Agricultural Workforce Solutions

Designed for agricultural employers facing domestic labor shortages. The H-2A program allows for the hiring of foreign workers for temporary or seasonal agricultural jobs, ensuring your operations continue smoothly from planting to harvest.

H-2B Visa

Non-Agricultural Labor Needs

Essential for non-agricultural sectors like landscaping, hospitality, and construction needing temporary workers for seasonal, peak load, or intermittent needs. Access foreign labor when qualified U.S. workers are unavailable.
