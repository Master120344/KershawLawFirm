The H-2A Visa: Cultivating Agricultural Success

Specifically designed for agricultural employers facing domestic worker shortages, the H-2A program allows the temporary admission of foreign workers for seasonal or temporary agricultural jobs. From planting to harvest, livestock care to specialized farming tasks, securing H-2A workers ensures your agricultural commitments are met with a capable, legal workforce.

The H-2B Visa: Powering Non-Agricultural Industries

For businesses in landscaping, hospitality, construction, forestry, manufacturing, and other non-agricultural sectors with seasonal, peak load, intermittent, or one-time needs, the H-2B visa is crucial. It provides access to foreign workers when qualified U.S. workers are unavailable, enabling you to meet peak demand and fulfill crucial contracts without interruption.
