What is the H-2A Visa Program?

The H-2A program allows U.S. agricultural employers to bring foreign nationals to the U.S. for seasonal or temporary agricultural jobs. This program ensures farmers and businesses have the workforce they need while protecting workers’ rights and maintaining compliance with federal law.

How the Process Works

  1. Determine Eligibility: We assess your business’s eligibility and job requirements.
  2. Submit Application: We file necessary paperwork with the Department of Labor and USCIS.
  3. Recruit Workers: Employers can recruit qualified foreign workers or we can help facilitate.
  4. Approval and Arrival: Upon approval, workers receive visas and arrive for work.
