H2B Visa Program

The H2B program allows U.S. employers to hire foreign workers for temporary non-agricultural jobs, such as hospitality, construction, and landscaping. This program helps businesses fill roles when they can't find enough local workers.

Who Can Apply?

The H2B visa is for employers, not workers. Employers must prove they need temporary labor and that no qualified U.S. workers are available for the job.

How Kershaw Law Firm Helps

Our firm assists employers in navigating the H2B application process, ensuring compliance with immigration law and maximizing the chances of approval. We handle filing, documentation, and legal guidance.

Benefits of the H2B Program

  • Hire skilled foreign workers for up to 9 months.
  • Quick application turnaround.
  • Stay fully compliant with U.S. immigration regulations.
