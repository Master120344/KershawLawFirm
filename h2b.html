What is an H-2B Visa?

The H-2B visa allows U.S. employers to **strategically** hire foreign nationals for temporary non-agricultural jobs. This program is designed for companies that experience **seasonal, peak-load, or intermittent labor shortages**, ensuring they remain competitive and **never lose their edge** in the market.
