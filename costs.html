Costs & Process

Understand the fees and steps involved in getting your workforce legally.

Costs Breakdown

  • Legal Consultation: Free initial consultation
  • Application Preparation: $1,500 - $2,500
  • Filing Fees: $460 USCIS fee + $150 DOL fee
  • Recruitment Costs: Varies based on location & duration
  • Total Estimated Cost: Starting from $2,500+

Process Overview

We handle everything from paperwork to ensuring compliance:

  • Step 1: Employer Consultation & Needs Assessment
  • Step 2: Filing with the Department of Labor (DOL)
  • Step 3: Filing with U.S. Citizenship & Immigration Services (USCIS)
  • Step 4: Worker Consulate Processing
  • Step 5: Arrival & Compliance Monitoring
