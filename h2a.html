What is the H-2A Visa?

The H-2A visa program allows U.S. agricultural employers to hire foreign workers for temporary or seasonal jobs when domestic labor is scarce. Updated for 2025, it features streamlined processes, enhanced worker protections, and compliance with the latest DOL regulations.

Benefits of the H-2A Program

Reliable Workforce

Access skilled workers during peak seasons to maintain productivity.

Legal Compliance

Ensure adherence to federal labor laws with a structured visa process.

Cost Efficiency

Save on Social Security and Medicare taxes for H-2A employees.

Employer Responsibilities

  • Job Eligibility: Demonstrate a shortage of U.S. workers.
  • Wages: Pay the highest of AEWR, state/federal minimum, or prevailing wage (2025 rates).
  • Housing & Travel: Provide free housing and transportation.
  • Safety: Comply with updated DOL health and safety standards.
