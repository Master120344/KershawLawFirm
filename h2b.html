What is the H-2B Visa?

The H-2B visa program allows U.S. employers to hire foreign workers for temporary non-agricultural jobs, such as hospitality, landscaping, and construction. As of 2025, the program includes updated caps and streamlined processes to meet seasonal and peak-load demands.

Benefits of the H-2B Program

Reliable Workforce

Fill labor gaps during peak seasons with skilled temporary workers.

Legal Compliance

Maintain compliance with federal regulations through a structured visa process.

Flexibility

Address seasonal, peak-load, or one-time staffing needs efficiently.

Employer Responsibilities

  • Job Eligibility: Demonstrate a temporary need and lack of available U.S. workers.
  • Wages: Pay the highest of prevailing, federal, or state minimum wage (2025 rates apply).
  • Worker Protections: Ensure no displacement of U.S. workers and compliance with labor laws.
  • Return Transportation: Cover return travel costs for workers completing their contract.
