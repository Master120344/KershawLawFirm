What is an H-2A Visa?

The **H-2A visa** allows **U.S. agricultural employers** to **legally** hire foreign workers for **temporary or seasonal farm work**. Under **8 U.S. Code § 1101**, this program ensures **compliance** with labor laws while meeting the demands of American agriculture.
