What is an H-2A Visa?

The H-2A visa allows U.S. agricultural employers to bring foreign workers for temporary or seasonal jobs when domestic workers are unavailable.

Eligibility Requirements

Employers must demonstrate a genuine need for seasonal agricultural workers and meet all labor and wage regulations set by the Department of Labor.
